Required text
-------------

\[SPP\] Silver, Edward, David Pyke, and Rein Peterson. _Inventory Management and Production Planning and Scheduling_. 3rd ed. New York, NY: John Wiley & Sons, 1998. ISBN: 9780471119470.

--------------------------------------------------------
